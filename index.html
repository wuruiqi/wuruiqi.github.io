I am a Lecturer and M.S. supervisor at Henan University of Technology, working on intelligent perception & decision-making and embodied AI. My current research focuses on visual SLAM, 3D scene reconstruction, and multi-level perception for smart grain silos. My research interests span intelligent control, intelligent robotics, deep reinforcement learning, and adversarial learning. I have led a Henan Provincial Natural Science Foundation project, a Henan Provincial Key Science & Technology project, an open project of the MOE Key Laboratory of Grain Information Processing & Control, and others, and have published 10+ papers in venues such as IEEE TII, Pattern Recognition, and Information Sciences.

河南工业大学讲师、硕士研究生导师，主要从事智能感知与决策和具身智能方向的研究。 目前具体在研课题包括视觉 SLAM、三维场景重建和智慧粮仓多层次感知。 研究方向涵盖智能控制、智能机器人、深度强化学习、对抗学习。近年来主持河南省自然科学基金青年项目、 河南省科技攻关项目、教育部粮食信息处理与控制重点实验室开放课题、河南省高等学校重点科研项目等多项课题， 在 IEEE TII、Pattern Recognition、Information Sciences 等重要期刊与国际会议上发表论文十余篇。

Prospective students招生说明

Our group works on intelligent perception & decision-making and embodied AI — concretely, visual SLAM, 3D scene reconstruction, and smart grain silos. We are a small team that builds things shoulder to shoulder, not a place that hands out assignments.

What you get by joining:

  • The chance to work on real, frontier topics — involved end to end, from problem selection to publication;
  • One-on-one, hands-on mentoring that quickly sharpens your skills in research, reading, writing, coding, and presentation;
  • A proven set of research methods and habits of mind that save you from detours;
  • Research stipends when funding allows, and support to attend academic conferences at home and abroad;
  • Genuine help and recommendations for your long-term path — PhD, study abroad, top tech companies, or a tech venture.

You may be a good fit if you:

  • Are curious about open problems and willing to spend a few years doing research seriously;
  • Are self-driven, reliable, and willing to dig in and try things yourself when stuck;
  • Have a solid math background and programming ability (C/C++, Python, or MATLAB), and can read English literature reasonably well;
  • Want to graduate with results that stand up to scrutiny — not just a diploma.

If your main plan is to coast to a degree while focusing on side jobs or preparing for civil-service exams, we are probably not the best fit — nothing to do with how capable you are, just a difference in direction, and saying so honestly is fair to us both.

Interested? Email rqwu@haut.edu.cn (Ruiqi Wu) early with a brief self-introduction, why you want to pursue graduate study, and your plans. Slots are limited each year — looking forward to meeting you.

我们课题组做智能感知与决策、具身智能，具体方向是视觉 SLAM、3D 场景重建和智慧粮仓。 这里更像一个并肩做事的小团队，而不是发任务的工位。

加入我们，你会得到：

  • 参与真实、前沿课题的机会，从选题、攻关到发表全程深度参与；
  • 导师一对一、手把手地带，快速提升你做研究、读文献、写论文、编程和表达的能力；
  • 一套被反复验证有效的科研方法和思维方式，让你少走弯路；
  • 经费允许时的科研补助，以及资助参加国内外学术会议；
  • 对你长远发展（读博、出国深造、进大厂、技术创业）的真诚帮助与推荐。

我们更适合这样的你：

  • 对未知问题有好奇心，愿意把科研当成一件值得投入几年去认真做的事；
  • 自驱、靠谱、肯钻研，遇到难题愿意自己先想办法；
  • 有较好的数学基础和编程能力（C/C++、Python、MATLAB 之一），能较顺利地读英文文献；
  • 希望毕业时能拿出经得起检验的成果，而不只是一纸文凭。

如果你只是想轻松拿个学位、把主要精力放在兼职或备考公务员/事业编上，那我们大概率不是最适合你的选择—— 这无关优秀与否，只是方向不同，坦诚说清，也是对彼此负责。

有兴趣的同学，欢迎尽早邮件联系 rqwu@haut.edu.cn（吴瑞琪）， 简单介绍一下你自己、为什么想读研、以及未来的打算。每年名额有限，期待与你相遇。
